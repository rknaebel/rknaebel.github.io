Biography

Hi! I have a B.Sc. in Business Informatics and an M.Sc. in Cognitive Systems, both from the University of Potsdam. My fascination for machine learning started growing during my master’s program. There I did several projects applied to natural sciences and linguistics. In my final research project, I got in touch with deep reinforcement learning in language-based games. For my master thesis, I applied neural networks for malware detection with the main aspect of investigating the influence of learning multiple tasks at once.

After finishing my Master, I started as as research assistant at the Machine Learning in Cognitive Sciences Lab of Sebastian Stober. My PhD is supervised by Manfred Stede. There, I work on the application of neural networks for discourse analysis.
